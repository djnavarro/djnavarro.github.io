About Me

Hi there! I'm a data scientist, generative artist, and a recovering academic currently living in Sydney with my two kids and a Netflix subscription. Once upon a time I was a mathematical psychologist. Then I was a developer advocate. After that I'll be something else I imagine. Somewhere in there I wrote a few books, and made a lot of art!

Data science blog

I write a data science blog, "Notes from a data witch". It covers a variety of topics including R, Python, Apache Arrow, Linux, and many other things related to data analysis workflows.

Generative art galleries

I have a generative art website where I post various art series under creative commons licences. For some series the source code is also available on github.
